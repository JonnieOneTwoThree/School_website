Knowledge is power

SCHOOL MOTTO:BIDII NA UAMINIFU

The Kabete National Polytechnic is a Government Institution of higher learning which was founded in 1924. The Institute has trained many graduates who are currently well placed in Public and Private Institutions and Organizations. The Institution is ISO 9001:2015 certified. We are proud to be the first TVET institution to Export Engineering Spare parts made in the college to the International market i.e. China. This is after having won in The Africa Tec Challenge (AFRITECH) sponsored by China. “Kabete National Polytechnic the home of Engineers, Technologies, Scientists, innovators and Business Entrepreneurs.”

The Kabete National Polytechnic Wikipedia. Overview of Kabete National Polytechnic according to Wikipedia The Kabete National Polytechnic is a national university offering technical training in Kenya. The Kabete technical is located on Waiyaki Way near the Uthiru market in Kiambu County.

Kabete national polytechnic is known countrywide for its best training and performance and is considered one of the best technical institutions in Kenya This institution was started in 1924 and underwent various revolutionary stages until 1974 where it was upgraded to a national polytechnic The institute is also accredited by TVET (Technical and Vocational Education and Training).
